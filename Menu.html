Eggs made as a result of mixing eggs with milk and butter

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Peper
  • Milk

Method

  1. Heat up the pan
  2. Add Milk to the eggs
  3. Add some butter to eggs
  4. Pour the mixture in the pan and stir
  5. cook for 4 minutes
